Privacy Policy

This privacy policy ("Policy") describes how we collect, protect, and use the personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") you ("User", "you" or "your") provide on the web application and any of its products or services (collectively, "Application").

1. Collection of personal information

The Application (snapme) collects certain Personal Information from you when you sign in using social login methods such as Facebook, Google, Twitter, and Instagram. This information may include your name, username, email address, and profile picture.

2. Use of collected information

The Application uses the collected Personal Information to authenticate and authorize users, and to provide the services and features offered through the Application. We may also use the Personal Information to contact you with newsletters, marketing or promotional materials and other information that may be of interest to you.

3. Protection of personal information

The Application implements appropriate measures to protect the security and confidentiality of the Personal Information we collect, includingthe use of secure servers and encryption. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

4. Sharing of personal information

The Application may share your Personal Information with third parties for the purpose of providing the services and features offered through the Application. We may also share your Personal Information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect and defend the rights or property of the Application, or protect the personal safety of users of the Application or the public.

5. Third-party services

The Application may use third-party service providers to service and support the Application, or to help us understand the use of the Application. These third-party service providers will have access to your Personal Information only as necessary to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

6. Data retention

The Application will retain your Personal Information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Policy. We will retain and use your Personal Information to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations (for example, if we are required to retain your data to comply with applicable laws), resolve disputes, and enforce our legal agreements and policies.

7. Changes to the policy

The Application may update this Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Policy, please contact us.
